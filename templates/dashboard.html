The personal page of Tyler J. Skluzacek.

My name is Tyler J. Skluzacek, and I'm a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Chicago where I'm a member of Globus Labs and RDCEP. My research strives to produce generalized methods for indexing petascale heterogeneous, scientific repositories. This work lies at the nexus of cloud computing, machine learning, file forensics, and search.

I recently completed an internship with IBM Research where I developed modular, scalable genome assembly pipelines for IBM's OMXWare.
